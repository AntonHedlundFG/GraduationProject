Unit Design Philosophy

Cleric:
A mixture of healing/support/and Meele/Range focused abilites, nothing to powerfull damage wise unless its cleric specific

Monk:
Has a mixture of strong meele focused abilities/utility, also has a few support/minor healing abilities

Warrior:
Strong focus on meele abilites, also has access to better armor abilities, TANKS

Ranger:
Strong ranged attacks (not spells), focuses on moveability and utility

Mage:
Strong spellcaster with limited movement

Necromancer: 
Shares ability pool with some of the mage abilities, mainly fire, but also has a focus on spells that damages and heals self, 
Uses selfish means to achieve victory. Think, puts curses on enemies. Summons?
